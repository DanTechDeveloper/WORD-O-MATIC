# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

K-5 students are the primary audience: young children practicing reading and
speaking in a gamified environment, usually on classroom desktops or tablets
with teacher supervision. Teachers are the secondary audience: they manage
classes, assign work, review progress, and award recognition. The auth/home
surface may speak to teachers or parents, but the product's heart is the
student game.

## Product Purpose

WordOMatic is a gamified reading and speaking practice platform for early-grade
students. It turns foundational literacy drills (word recognition, reading
aloud, speaking) into an arcade-style game with levels, badges, streaks,
leaderboards, and avatars. Teachers set up classes and assignments and track
outcomes. Success means a child who chooses to keep practicing, and a teacher
who can see real progress.

## Positioning

Practice that feels like play: the only literacy game where young kids choose
to keep going.

## Brand Personality

Playful, electric, bold. Arcade energy without losing clarity; confident and a
little mischievous, never childish or corporate.

## Anti-references

- Glassmorphism AI-slop: blurred glass cards, gradient-clipped text, generic
  AI-generated sheen.
- Generic corporate edtech SaaS: blue/white admin dashboards, stock
  illustrations, flat minimalism.
- Infantilized "baby" styling: overly cartoonish, low-contrast,
  undifferentiated.

## Design Principles

- Tactile over flat: hard offset shadows and physical press feedback make every
  control feel like a real arcade button.
- Show, don't tell: teach through gameplay and feedback, not instructions.
- Forgiving and big: large tap targets and generous hit areas suit small hands
  and short attention.
- Electric focus: one action color carries intent; everything else is chrome.
- Celebrate progress: every win (streak, badge, level) earns a moment, not just
  a number.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast baseline on the dark canvas.
- Large, easy tap targets for young children on touchscreens.
- Honor reduced-motion: every animation has a calm fallback.
- Color-blind safe: keep the action color distinguishable from the
  violet / magenta / peach chrome.
